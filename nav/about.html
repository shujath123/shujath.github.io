“We ought to be about something beyond moving chicken.
We ought to be a piece of our client’s lives and the networks in which we serve.”
Our originator, settled on the choice to close on Sundays in 1946
when he opened his first eatery in Hyderabad,Telangana. Having worked seven days in
eateries open 24 hours, Truett saw the significance of shutting on Sundays with the
goal that he and his representatives could set aside one day to rest and love whether
they pick – a training we maintain today.
